About Us

At The Bored Gamer we are passionate about bringing people together through the joy of board games. Nestled in the heart of Cardiff, our cozy cafe provides a welcoming almosphere where friends, families and fellow gamers can gather to enjoy great food, drinks and, of course, an extensive collection of board games.
Whether you are a seasoned strategist or a casual player looking for some fun, our diverse selection ensures there is always something new to discover.
But The Bored Gamer is more than just a place to play games. It is a community hub where friendships are forged and memories are made.
Our friendly staff are always on hand to help you choose the perfect game, settle any rule discputes, or simply share the excitement of your latest victory!
